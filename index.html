Frequently Asked Questions

Does ReadyShipper offer the same level of functionality as ShipStation? expand_more
Yes. ReadyShipper supports multi-carrier shipping, automated rules, rate shopping, batch label printing, returns management, order syncing from all major marketplaces and carts, and much more. Most ShipStation users find that ReadyShipper matches or exceeds the functionality they relied on, with better performance and hands-on support.
Does ReadyShipper support multi-carrier rate shopping? expand_more
Absolutely. ReadyShipper can rate shop across multiple carriers and even across multiple accounts from the same carrier simultaneously. You can build custom rules that automatically select the cheapest or fastest option based on weight, destination, dimensions, and more.
How long does it take to move from ShipStation to ReadyShipper? expand_more
Most merchants are fully operational the same day. Our team handles your entire setup and migration in a single virtual session — including connecting your sales channels, configuring carrier accounts, and building your shipping rules and automations.
Does ReadyShipper support international shipping? expand_more
Yes. ReadyShipper supports international shipping with all major carriers including UPS, FedEx, DHL, and USPS. It handles customs forms, harmonized tariff codes, commercial invoices, and all required international documentation automatically.
Can ReadyShipper integrate with my ERP? expand_more
Yes. ReadyShipper supports ODBC (Open Database Connectivity), which allows direct integration with your existing ERP, WMS, or database systems. We also offer a shipping API and can connect to a wide range of enterprise platforms.
Does ReadyShipper support custom packing slips? expand_more
Yes. ReadyShipper supports fully customizable packing slips with your branding, logos, and custom fields. You can create multiple templates and automatically assign them based on your shipping rules.
Does ReadyShipper support hazmat and dangerous goods shipping? expand_more
Yes. ReadyShipper supports hazmat and dangerous goods shipping through carriers that offer it, including UPS and FedEx. The system can automatically flag hazmat items and apply the correct handling requirements to ensure compliance.
Does ReadyShipper support direct carrier integrations? expand_more
Yes. ReadyShipper connects directly to carriers like UPS, FedEx, USPS, DHL, and many regional and LTL carriers. This means no middleman markups on your postage — you pay the carrier directly at your negotiated rates.
How does ReadyShipper pricing work? expand_more
ReadyShipper uses flat-rate, per-user pricing starting at $64/month. There are no volume fees, no per-label fees, no credit card processing fees, and no transaction costs. You pay the same amount whether you ship 100 or 100,000 packages per month.
Does ReadyShipper have discounted carrier rates? expand_more
Yes. ReadyShipper offers access to discounted USPS rates and other carrier discounts. You can also bring your own carrier accounts with your own negotiated rates — there are no BYOA fees or markups.
What's the difference between ReadyShipper and ShipStation's customer support model? expand_more
ReadyShipper provides live video calls with screen sharing, U.S.-based phone support, email support, and hands-on onboarding where our team actually sets up your account with you. ShipStation relies primarily on email-based ticket support and self-service documentation.
Can I use my own carrier rates? expand_more
Yes. ReadyShipper lets you connect your existing UPS, FedEx, USPS, DHL, and other carrier accounts directly. You keep your negotiated rates and there are no BYOA surcharges or markup fees.
What automations does ReadyShipper support? expand_more
ReadyShipper supports unlimited automations including automatic carrier and service selection, package type assignment, insurance rules, delivery confirmation settings, order splitting and combining, custom tagging, and more. Rules can be based on weight, dimensions, destination, order value, SKU, channel, and dozens of other criteria.
What types of reporting does ReadyShipper support? expand_more
ReadyShipper provides comprehensive shipping analytics including cost-per-shipment reports, carrier performance tracking, volume trends, delivery time analysis, and custom dashboards. Premium plans include editable reports, scheduled reports, and alert-based notifications.
What types of integrations does ReadyShipper support? expand_more
ReadyShipper integrates with all major ecommerce platforms (Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, Magento), marketplaces (Amazon, eBay, Walmart, Etsy), ERP systems via ODBC, and 100+ carriers. A shipping API is also available for custom integrations.
Does ReadyShipper automatically import and sync orders? expand_more
Yes. ReadyShipper provides automatic 2-way order syncing with all connected sales channels. Orders are imported in real time, and tracking information and shipment status are automatically pushed back to your stores and marketplaces.
Will ReadyShipper help me ship faster than ShipStation? expand_more
Yes. ReadyShipper's hybrid-cloud architecture lets you batch print up to 4 labels per second — roughly 400% faster than browser-based platforms like ShipStation. Combined with automated rule-based shipping decisions, your team spends less time on each order.
Does ReadyShipper help me stay compliant with carriers? expand_more
Yes. ReadyShipper helps you stay compliant with carrier requirements including address validation, hazmat documentation, dimensional weight calculations, and packaging rules. The system can automatically apply the correct settings based on your products and destinations, reducing the risk of surcharges and delivery issues.
