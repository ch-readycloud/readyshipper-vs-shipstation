Frequently Asked Questions

Does ReadyShipper offer the same level of functionality as ShipStation? expand_more
Yes — and then some. ReadyShipper matches ShipStation's core shipping functionality while delivering significantly more speed and cost savings. With ReadyCloud, you also get a full ecommerce suite spanning shipping, fulfillment, returns, tracking, and post-purchase email/SMS, along with organization-wide permissions and rules.

Where ShipStation falls short is speed. Being browser-based limits how fast labels can be generated, which means you may need to hire more staff just to keep up with volume. ShipStation also charges per label when using your own negotiated carrier rates, bills for user seats whether they're actively used or not, and gates key features like API access and phone support behind higher-priced plans.
How long does it take to move from ShipStation to ReadyShipper? expand_more
Most customers switch and go live the same day. That includes hands-on setup, rule building, sales channel configuration, and everything in between. More complex environments — such as ERP integrations with NetSuite — typically take a week or less.
What's the difference between ReadyShipper and ShipStation's customer support? expand_more
ReadyShipper offers live chat, email, phone, and live meeting support with screen share — so you always have a real person available when you need one. ShipStation limits phone support to Standard and Premium plans, and does not offer live meeting or screen share support at any tier.
Will ReadyShipper help me ship faster than ShipStation? expand_more
Yes. ReadyShipper is a hybrid-cloud application built for speed in high-volume environments. It batch prints up to 4 labels per second, scans to print in under 2 seconds, and syncs orders in real time with two-way updates. Shipping decisions and rate shopping are fully automated, so your team spends less time making manual choices and more time getting orders out the door. ShipStation's browser-based architecture simply can't match this output, often requiring additional staff to hit the same throughput.
What automations does ReadyShipper support? expand_more
ReadyShipper supports a wide range of automations, including shipping rules, order importing, multi-carrier rate shopping, order splitting and combining, order routing, real-time order status sync, post-purchase email and SMS notifications, conveyor and machine integrations, hazmat and dangerous goods handling, and address validation.
What does ReadyShipper integrate with? expand_more
ReadyShipper connects with ecommerce carts and sales channels, direct carrier integrations, ERPs, TMS platforms, and custom API setups. It also supports legacy data formats including ODBC and CSV, making it compatible with virtually any existing workflow or tech stack.
How does ReadyShipper pricing work? expand_more
ReadyShipper is priced per user — that's it. There are no volume tiers, no per-label fees, no transaction fees, and no credit card surcharges. You can bring your own negotiated carrier rates at no additional cost, and you also get access to ReadyShipper's pre-negotiated discounted carrier rates right out of the box.
Does ReadyShipper support multi-carrier rate shopping? expand_more
Yes. ReadyShipper's Rate Genie automatically shops rates across multiple carriers on every shipment. You can optimize for the cheapest rate, the fastest delivery, or the best value based on cost relative to time in transit. Rate shopping decisions can also be fully automated through your shipping rules, so the right carrier is always selected without any manual intervention.
