Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I realistically save by switching? expand_more
Most high-volume merchants (5k+ shipments/mo) save between $1,200 and $4,500 monthly by eliminating volume surcharges and hidden transaction fees that legacy platforms tuck into their "partnerships."
Are there really no credit card processing fees? expand_more
Correct. ReadyShipper is built on a direct carrier-integration model. We do not skim percentages off your shipping labels. You pay your carrier directly, and you pay us a flat software subscription.
How long does migration from ShipStation take? expand_more
Our migration wizard handles the bulk of data in minutes. With our white-glove setup, most businesses are fully operational with their custom workflows, rules, and automations within 24-48 hours.
Which carriers do you support? expand_more
We support all major global carriers including UPS, FedEx, USPS (via Stamps.com or Endicia), DHL, and over 100+ LTL and regional carriers worldwide.
What's included in ReadyShipper's assisted onboarding? expand_more
For merchants shipping 2,200+ packages monthly, we provide full white-glove onboarding — including connecting all your sales channels, building custom shipping rules and automations, and going live. Most merchants are fully operational the same day.
Does ReadyShipper charge per-label or volume-based fees? expand_more
Never. Our pricing is flat-rate based on your subscription tier. Whether you ship 100 or 100,000 labels in a month, your ReadyShipper cost stays the same. The only variable is user seats.
Can I bring my own carrier accounts? expand_more
Absolutely. ReadyShipper lets you connect your existing UPS, FedEx, USPS, DHL, and other carrier accounts directly — with no BYOA surcharges or markup fees. You keep your negotiated rates.
What is ODBC and is it included? expand_more
ODBC (Open Database Connectivity) allows ReadyShipper to connect directly to your existing databases, ERPs, and warehouse management systems for seamless data exchange. It's included at no extra cost on our Premium plan.
